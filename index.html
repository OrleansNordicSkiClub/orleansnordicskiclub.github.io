Greenbelt Cross Country Ski – Grooming and Track-setting

Are you generally happy with the trail grooming and track-setting done by the ONSC at Pine Grove, Mer Bleue and the Hornet’s Nest/Green’s Creek?

The National Capital Commission has entered into an agreement with the ONSC permitting the grooming and track-setting of classic XC ski trails and gives the ONSC $625 to help off-set the costs (this represents about 7% of the annual $9,000 cost of grooming and track-setting; the ONSC absorbs all other costs). The ONSC is always appreciative of this and any support.

The City supports the agreement by clearing the parking lots used by XC skiers and others.

Both the NCC and the City need to hear about what you think of the service provided - at no cost to the user.
